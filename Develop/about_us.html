How to join team?


If you have a passion for learning, collaborating
with talented individuals, and honing your software development
skills, you've come to the right place!

How to join our team and become a contributor:

1.Explore our repositories on GitHub: [link to your GitHub organization/profile].
2.Find a project that interests you and review the contribution guidelines.
3.Make your changes or new features to the project.
4.Propose your changes through a pull request on GitHub.
5.Our team will review your contribution and get in touch with you for further collaboration.

Don't worry if you're new to Git and GitHub or have limited
experience. We are ready to provide assistanceand support
to help you quickly get up to speed with the contribution process.

We welcome new ideas and perspectives, and your
contributions are incredibly valuable to us.
Together, we can create something truly special!
